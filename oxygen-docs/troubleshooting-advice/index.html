Troubleshooting Advice

This section includes suggestions and tips for navigating through potential issues, and getting the most out of Oxygen.​

Minimum Requirements​

To avoid any unnecessary difficulties, first make sure your hardware and software meet the following minimum requirements:

• Apple software: iOS 12 or newer.

• Apple hardware: Any device capable of running at least iOS 12. More information can be seen at: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IOS_12#Supported_devices

• Personal computer software: The Google Chrome browser is recommended.

• Personal computer hardware: Any Mac or PC computer produced after 2012 will be fine.

Note: It is always recommended to use the latest MacOS / Android software for smart devices, and the latest version of Google Chrome on your desktop computer.

Initial Recommendations​

1. If something doesn’t seem to work correctly, the first rule is to log out of the web-app and log in again. For example: if you're logged in and clicking something (such as 'Home' or Dashboard') doesn't work, logging out of the app and back in again may fix it.

2. If problems continue, try shutting down the Oxygen mini-server and restarting it.

General Usage​

• If an iPad or computer has previously connected to other wireless networks, there is a chance the device could switch its network connection without warning if one of these other networks is available. So if the web-app suddenly stops working, a good first step is to verify that your iPad is still connected to the Oxygen wireless network.

• With a computer, when connecting for the first time to https://oxygen.mystatbridge.com/ in a browser window, it may take slightly longer to display the 'Login' screen than usual. If it still has not loaded after 10 seconds, refresh the browser window and try again.

• If an iPad is not used for a while and the screen goes dark, resuming use will bring up the screen as it appeared previously. However, there is a chance that certain functions inside the administration Dashboard may not display 100% correctly (for example, some previously selected data may not appear in a form). If this occurs, logging out of the web-app and back in again will reset things to the correct state.

• If the web-app is launched on an iPad, and you upload an eligibility file via your computer, upon returning to your iPad the uploaded eligibility file may not appear. If so, you will need to log out of the web-app and back in again to see the new file.

Dashboard > Screenings​

• On the 'Add/Edit Screening' form, a selection must be made for "Client" before the available options will appear for 'Blueprint' and 'Eligibility File'. So if these selectors aren't showing the options you’re looking for, ensure that the correct 'Client' is selected.

Data Collection Screens​

• It may occasionally occur that when clicking a response option on a form (for example, ‘Yes’ or ‘No’ buttons), the answer may not be recorded immediately on the device (i.e. a checkmark appears next to the answer). This is because any selection on a device needs to be passed wirelessly back to the mini-server to be recorded, and sometimes wi-fi connections can vary in the amount of time needed to transfer data. This normally happens instantaneously. But if you ever click (or tap) a response and the form doesn’t show your answer as immediately recorded, you can simply click/tap on the button again until the response is locked in.

Further Assistance​

If there are any questions or issues in using the Oxygen System, you may e-mail support @ statbridge.com for additional assistance.
