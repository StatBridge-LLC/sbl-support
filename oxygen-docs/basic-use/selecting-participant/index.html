Selecting A Participant

This section describes searching, selecting and adding of participants, allowing them to continue through the screening.​

To access this page, click “Home” on the Dashboard. This will load the ‘Select A Participant’ page.

Searching For A Participant​

Note: The following assumes a screening event is active, and an eligibility file has been attached to the screening.

1. In the field below ‘Select A Participant’, start typing the first characters of the participant’s first or last name. You can also search by typing the characters of a participant's Unique ID into the field. Any matching results will appear below.

2. When the desired participant appears, click anywhere on the corresponding row to select them.

→ The ‘Verify Participant’ page for that participant will be loaded.

Additional Notes:

• You may search by first and last name if you place a space between the names.

• The order you enter the first or last names does not matter.

Adding A New Participant​

If you cannot find a participant, you may add them. Please make sure first that the participant is not in the search results. Try variations of their first and last names to check all possibilities.

1. Click the “+” button to the right of the search field.

2. On the resulting ‘Edit Participant’ form, fill in all required fields to match the participant’s information. It’s recommended to also fill in the unrequired fields for a more complete data entry.

Note: For ‘Unique ID’, it’s recommended to use an alphanumeric code at least 9 digits in length. Codes created by a random password generator are ideal, and U.S. social security numbers will also work.

3. When finished, click “Save”.

→ The ‘Select A Participant’ page will be loaded.

Participant Statuses​

Participants returned in a search will have a green, orange, or red circle beside their name. These colors indicate the following:

• Green means no data has been collected, and they are eligible to be added to the screening.

• Orange means they have been added to the screening, and are ready for data collection.

• Red means they have completed the screening, and are now closed out.
