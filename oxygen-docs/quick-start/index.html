Quick Start Guide

This section describes starting up the Oxygen system, pairing PTS CardioChek Plus meters, and shutting down.​

A. System Start-up​

1. Plug the Oxygen mini-server’s power cord into a power source.

2. Plug the tp-link box’s power cord into a power source.

3. In about 30 seconds, the Ubiquity access point (saucer) will emit a solid blue glow on top. This means the wi-fi signal is transmitting and ready for use.

4. Turn on the mini-server by pressing the power button on top of the box.

5. After a few moments, the power button will glow and the mini-server will be ready for use.

B. Connecting to Oxygen with a Personal Computer​

1. With the Oxygen system running, view the available Wi-Fi networks on your computer and join the ‘Oxygen xxxx’ network for your system (xxxx is unique to each system).

2. Enter the Wi-Fi network password (provided to you by StatBridge), and connect.

3. Launch the Chrome browser, and navigate to https://oxygen.mystatbridge.com in the URL address field. The Oxygen login screen will be loaded.

C. Oxygen Start-up​

1. On the Oxygen login screen, enter a manager’s Username and Password and click ‘Submit’.

2. You will now see the ‘Activate Database’ screen. Enter the activation (or decryption) password for your system, and click ‘Submit’. The message “Database activated…” will appear, and Oxygen is now ready for use.

D. Pairing a CardioChek Plus Meter to the Oxygen System​

1. With the Oxygen system running, plug the USB connector on the supplied CardioChek Plus USB cable into one of the mini-server’s USB ports.

2. Power on the CardioChek Plus device as per normal.

3. Now remove the MEMo chip from the top of the CardioChek Plus meter, and insert the other end of the supplied USB cable into that port.

4. After about 10 seconds, the CardioChek Plus meter will power off. When this happens, it has been successfully associated with the system.

5. Unplug the USB cable from the CardioChek Plus meter, and plug the ‘MEMO CHIP’ back in.

6. When the CardioChek Plus meter is powered on again, it will now automatically connect to the Oxygen system.

Note: To confirm that a meter is connected wirelessly to the mini-server, look for an icon showing wi-fi signal bars in the upper right of the PTS meter's screen. If you see this icon, the meter is connected successfully.

E. System Shutdown​

1. When ready to shut down, press the glowing power button on top of the mini-server. NOTE: It is recommended to conclude any active screening event prior to shutting the system down.

2. When the power button light stops glowing, the mini-server has been powered off completely.

3. To shut down the Ubiquity access point (saucer), unplug the tp-link box’s power cord from the power source.

Further Information​

The above information covers the basic setup and use of the Oxygen hardware and software. You are now ready to proceed with other aspects of the application for administration and screening.
